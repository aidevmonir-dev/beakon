Welcome to the Beakon preview

Thanks for taking a look. This is a private, time-limited demo running over a secure tunnel. Below is everything you need to sign in.

1. Open the preview

Open this link in any modern browser (Chrome, Edge, Safari, Firefox):

https://patricia-cameron-affordable-beyond.trycloudflare.com/

First-time tip: Cloudflare may show a short "You are about to visit…" interstitial. Click Visit Site to continue. This is normal for tunnel preview links.

2. Create your account

We recommend creating your own account Recommended so you experience Beakon the same way a real client would — your own workspace, your own organisation, the full guided onboarding.

  1. From the landing page click Sign Up (top right), or go straight to /register.
  2. Enter your work email and click Continue.
  3. You'll see "Create your account". Fill in:
    • First name and Last name
    • A password (at least 8 characters)
  4. Click Create account. You'll be signed in automatically and dropped into the onboarding wizard.
30-day trial · no credit card · change plan anytime. Picking a plan first on the Pricing page is optional — you can also start without one and choose later from inside the app.

3. Complete the onboarding

After sign-up, Beakon walks you through a short setup so the workspace feels like yours:

  1. Organisation — name, country, base currency.
  2. Accounting setup — pick a chart-of-accounts template (or import your own).
  3. Entity structure — add the legal entities you'd like to model.
  4. Launcher — eight tiles open into the modules; pick one to start exploring.

Once that's done you're inside the real product, with your own data.

4. Where to look first

A good 5-minute tour after onboarding:

  1. Overview — home dashboard, KPIs and recent activity.
  2. Journal Entries — AI-assisted posting (starts with the Transaction Type picker).
  3. Bills and Invoices — AP/AR powered by the same engine.
  4. Approvals — bookkeeper review queue.
  5. Reports — Trial Balance, P&L, Balance Sheet.
